Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering

Duration: 4 years

Graduate Programs

Master of Science in Computer Science

Duration: 2 years

Overview: This program is intended for students who wish to pursue careers in industry or advanced research while broadening their understanding of computer science.

Ph.D. in Computer Science

Duration: Varies

Overview: Students interested in performing original research in computer science and making major contributions to the area should apply to the Ph.D. program.
