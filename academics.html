Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Duration: 4 years

Overview: This program provides a comprehensive education in computer science, covering topics such as algorithms, programming, and software engineering.

Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering

Duration: 4 years

Overview: Focused on the principles and practices of software engineering, this program prepares students for a career in designing and building software systems.

Graduate Programs

Master of Science in Computer Science

Duration: 2 years

Overview: This program is designed for students who want to deepen their knowledge in computer science and pursue advanced research or industry positions.

Ph.D. in Computer Science

Duration: Varies

Overview: The Ph.D. program is for students interested in conducting original research in computer science and making significant contributions to the field.
